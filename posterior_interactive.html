Posterior distributions of party-mean ideal points (θ̄) from 800 MCMC draws. Each curve is a kernel density estimate of the posterior distribution of a party's average legislator position. Shaded regions mark the 95% credible interval.

Toggle parties:

800 posterior draws per party from a Bayesian 2-PL IRT model (brms/Stan, 2 chains × 800 iter, 400 warmup). Negative θ = coalition side; positive θ = opposition side. Bandwidth: Silverman's rule. The FDP's narrow posterior reflects exceptionally cohesive voting — its credible interval is the tightest of any party.
